透過系統中的uuid檔案，生成UUID
```bash
$ cat /proc/sys/kernel/random/uuid
371a8e69-d35c-4eb1-bc3c-36accca6a6a0
$ cat /proc/sys/kernel/random/uuid
0c1f3448-a089-4f4a-aaca-a7dc817f6cea
$ cat /proc/sys/kernel/random/uuid
8fff67aa-093d-441d-ae57-277ae5fb1bde
```
